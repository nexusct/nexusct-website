About NexusCT

Building Smarter Facilities Since 2016

NexusCT (Nexus Communications Technology) is a Schaumburg, IL-based technology integrator delivering enterprise-grade solutions to healthcare, real estate, hospitality, and construction industries.

Our Mission

Connecting Your World, Powering Your Future

Founded in 2016, NexusCT bridges the gap between complex technology requirements and real-world facility operations. We design, install, and manage systems that healthcare providers, property managers, and facility operators depend on every day.

Our approach is simple: understand our clients' operational challenges, engineer solutions that exceed expectations, and deliver ongoing support that ensures peak performance.

Our Vision

End-to-End Excellence

We envision a future where every facility — from a 20-bed assisted living community to a 500-unit REIT — operates on technology that is reliable, integrated, and professionally managed.
