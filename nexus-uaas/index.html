What We Deliver

Complete Building Technology

Five integrated service pillars — one unified platform, one monthly payment.

Managed Internet

Three tiers of dedicated internet connectivity tailored to your facility's bandwidth needs. We handle ISP negotiations, provisioning, and ongoing management so you never deal with an outage alone.

Structured Cabling & Rewiring

Complete infrastructure rewiring — Cat6A cabling, fiber backbone, structured MDF/IDF buildouts, and pathway management. We bring your building's physical layer into the modern era.

UniFi Network Backbone

Full Ubiquiti UniFi deployment — Dream Machine Pro gateways, managed PoE switches, and enterprise access points delivering seamless, high-density wireless coverage across your entire facility.

UniFi Protect Cameras

PoE-powered 4K camera systems with AI-driven detection, license plate recognition, facial recognition, night vision, and local NVR recording. No monthly cloud fees — your footage stays on-premise.

UniFi Access Control

State-of-the-art door access with NFC cards, biometric readers, time-based scheduling, and real-time alerts. Seamlessly integrated with UniFi Protect for video-verified entry events.
