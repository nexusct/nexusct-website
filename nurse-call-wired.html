Jeron Provider 700 & 790

Enterprise-grade wired nurse call systems meeting UL1069 and UL2560 standards. Designed for hospitals, skilled nursing facilities, and assisted living communities.

UL1069 Compliant

Reliable Wired Communication

The Jeron Provider 700 and 790 systems deliver the gold standard in wired nurse call technology. Every component is UL1069 and UL2560 listed, ensuring your facility meets all regulatory requirements.

Complete Feature Set

  • Bedside call stations with pillow speaker controls
  • Master nurse stations with full system visibility
