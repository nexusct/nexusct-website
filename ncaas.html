NCaaS

Nurse Call Systems-as-a-Service

Eliminate capital expenditure and get a fully managed nurse call system for a predictable monthly fee. All hardware, installation, support, and updates included.

Subscription Model

Eliminate CapEx. Predictable OpEx.

NCaaS transforms nurse call from a capital expense into a predictable operating expense. You get the latest technology without the upfront investment — plus 24/7 managed support, automatic updates, and guaranteed uptime.
