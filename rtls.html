RTLS & Wander Management

Real-Time Location Intelligence

Track assets, monitor patient flow, optimize staff allocation, and manage wander risk — all in real time with RFID and BLE technology from Accutech and Aero Wireless.

Technology

RFID + BLE Location Platform

Our RTLS platform combines RFID and Bluetooth Low Energy technologies to deliver precise indoor positioning. Track wheelchairs, IV pumps, patients, and staff across your entire facility in real time.

Integration with nurse call systems, EMR platforms, and building management enables automated workflows that reduce manual processes and improve care coordination.

Capabilities

What RTLS Delivers

  • Real-time asset tracking and utilization reports
  • Patient flow monitoring and throughput analysis
  • Staff allocation and proximity awareness
  • Wander management with zone-based alerts
  • Elopement prevention for memory care
  • EMR and nurse call integration
  • Building management system integration
  • Historical reporting and analytics
Use Cases

Asset Tracking

Wander Management
